PROJECT TO HELP CO-TU PEOPLE PRODUCE FOOD

The Pà-căng village has 30 Co-tu families, of which 26 families are poor (income less than 1,500,000 VND/household/month or 65 USD household/month). Each family has 4~6 people. Every day the people go to the forest or to the river to look for food or items that can be sold. During the rainy season (September ~ December),they often lack of food and clothing.Each family has a land space of about 500m2. It is almost vacant currently.

People grow some crops and raise some livestock according to old habits.They do not have access to science and technology for effective production. Most of them speak Co-tu language. All people in the village wish they can access farming techniques to develop their garden land for food.

Budget: 113,200,000 VND (4,800 USD)
